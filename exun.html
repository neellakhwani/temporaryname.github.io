Exun 2016

November 2016

Exun, the computer club at Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram, stands for Experts Unlimited.The club hosts an annual interschool symposium, also called Exun. As Creative Head for 2015-16, I was responsible for designing a coherent identity for the event. I decided to maintain consistency throughout the design by illustrating a mascot that would respresent the event, Exun 2016.

Figure 1 : The Onstage Banner

Having represented the school in various computing symposia in India and around the world, the club also hosts the Delhi Public School R.K. Puram annual computer symposium “Exun”. Fifteen such symposiums have taken place and have been a grand success. In 2016, Exun held its 21st Annual Symposium.

Figure 2 : The cover for the annual magazine
Figure 3 : The cover for the sponsor prospectus

The mascot lead to the entire event coming together and seeming like a well planned and designed symposium. This project helped me understand how important teamwork is and I also learned that design isn't just something that looks visually appealing, it is more about the ideation and how well planned and well implemented the idea is. Overall, it was a great experience and I loved every minute of it!
