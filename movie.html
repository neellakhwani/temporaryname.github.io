Movie Poster
14 May 2017
About the Project

This is a movie poster I had made during Access 2016. Access is the annual interschool IT Fest organised by Modern School, Barakhamba Road.

The task was to create a movie poster for a movie titled “Fake”.

The movie titled ‘Fake’ is based on how a fake note disturbs the relationship between two successful businessmen. In a general everyday deal, one of them discovers the note — put in by the other businessman — in the transaction and it leads to complexities between them.
