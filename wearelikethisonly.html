We are like this only

March 2018

The brief given by my college was to take inspiration from an existing designer's work and creating something of your own. I took Brown Paper Bag comics by Sailesh Gopalan as my inspiration. Sailesh Gopalan is a 20 year old design student at Srishti School of Art, Design and Technology. Initially started on 25th June, 2016, the webcomic has exploded phemonally.

His comic strips have a different story every time but the theme is the same - Indian satire. His minimal illustration style and how he manages to portray the message is something that really fascinates me.

Figure 1 : Inspiration and Mood Board

Once the concept was finalised, I moved on to creating my own characters. I started out by drawing rough sketches of how my characters could look. This part was a lot of fun but was also the most critical part of the entire project since it would decide what my stories would come off as to the readers. My drawing isn't that great so I mostly just did absolute basic sketches.

Figure 2 : Rough Sketches

After the entire process of digitalisation was done, I had to arrange for the printing. This involved calling up multiple printers, getting the costing, bargaining, making sure that I could afford to get the cards printed without spending all of my pocket money. After all the hardwork (with a hint of struggling), everything got printed and this is what it looked like.
